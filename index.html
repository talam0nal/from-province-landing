Sabotage

Start: August 2, 2024, 22:00 (UTC+3)
Venue: Kaluga, Yachenskaya Embankment, Park Mir

Dear friends, From Province and Resurrection Community Records are on air. We're throwing a multi-format electronic madness called Sabotage. On the night of August 2, 2024, we invite you to immerse in the atmosphere of electronic underground music right here on the bank of Yachenskoye Reservoir. Great music, a pleasant company, a scenic venue, high-quality sound and lighting - those are the key ingredients of our party.

On the event you can taste a diverse range of genres: Drum&Bass/Breakbeat/BassHouse/Techno and even Hardcore

We also have a special guest from Minsk, Slayt. And the father of the local bass scene, Hightech. We will also have there well-known representatives of the local bass scene: Undervog, AiMentor, Skart, Exhausted plus for the most resilient of you, a secret guest with a hardcore set.

About Us

We are a collaboration of DJs united by our passion for music and our will to ignite the dance floor. Our arsenal covers a diverse range of genres, from classic house to cutting-edge techno and drum-and-bass tracks. We are ready to deliver an unforgettable experience and fuel your energy!
