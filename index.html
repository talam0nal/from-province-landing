Sabotage

Dear friends, From Province and Resurrection Community Records are on air. We're throwing a multi-format electronic madness called Sabotage. On the night of August 2, 2024, we invite you to immerse in the atmosphere of electronic underground music right here on the bank of Yachenskoye Reservoir. Great music, a pleasant company, a scenic venue, high-quality sound and lighting - those are the key ingredients of our party.

On the event you can taste a diverse range of genres: Drum&Bass/Breakbeat/BassHouse/Techno and even Hardcore

We also have a special guest from Minsk, Slayt. And the father of the local bass scene, Hightech. We will also have there well-known representatives of the local bass scene: Undervog, AiMentor, Skart, Exhausted plus for the most resilient of you, a secret guest with a hardcore set.

Hightech

Vladimir Hightech is another headliner of our party, many of you may already know him for his powerful D&B sets. This night as always, Hightech will warm up the dance floor with his great performance including lots of exclusive tracks.

For those of you who don't know Vladimir, Higthech is a hugely experienced sound producer from Kaluga. His tracks were released under such Drum and Bass labels as: Close 2 Death Recordings (UK), Basspointz Recordings (GR), Modulate Recordings (ITA), Mind Outside Recordings (UA), TAM Records (RUS), Black Seeds Recordings (ITA), Ammunition Recordings (USA) etc.

About Us

We are a collaboration of DJs united by our passion for music and our will to ignite the dance floor. Our arsenal covers a diverse range of genres, from classic house to cutting-edge techno and drum-and-bass tracks. We are ready to deliver an unforgettable experience and fuel your energy!

Our Team

Vndzr

Vndzr is one of the founders of the label. For his 14th birthday he got a present of access to the world wide web. The time he spent there wasn't wasted, it put him up with speed break beats and rocking bass.

He visited multiple festivals taking up other people's experience to apply it to his own projects. He organised tens of events with the participation of famous russian producers of dnb/dubstep scene, he also took part in some of them as a performer.

By today, he works on his own tracks. Together with his partners in the label he he still runs organisation activities as "R.C.R."

Undervog

Yegor, a co-founder of the promo group From Province, a Dj and event organiser. He also works as a designer for Resurrection Community Records.

He has organised numerous local and big bass events in the Cradle of Cosmonautics, where he shared the stage with famous Russian producers such as Nphonix, Oiki, iBenji, Ozma etc.

Today, he applies his designer skills to the label Resurrection Community Records and at the same time works on the events organisation.

Undervog is like a scientist who went crazy and is still experimenting with different musical potions and serums. This time he invented something new and therapeutic but he needs test subjects to finish his experiments. Be warned that his remedy has a series of side effects: an increase in body temperature, sweating, hiperactivity, good mood etc.
